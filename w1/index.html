Master Mobile UX

Web developers are encouraged to follow the mobile-first-design thinking pattern. According to research, the sales for desktop is in constant decline while the demands for mobile gadgets is on the rise. Web developers should be observant rather than great listeners. Steve Jobs said, "A lot of times people don't know what they want until you show it to them." The user experience should include reachability, speed, and quick access to important navigation elements. Designs should be simple, intuitive, and mobile-friendly.
